How it works

When you upload a document to Drop, you will receive a link that you can send to your friends and they will be able to download your document! The link will expire after an hour so we can keep the service free and functional for everyone. No registration is required and you can upload documents that are not larger than 200 megabytes.

Upon uploading a document, the document is encrypted and saved on the server. If a data breach happens, you can be sure your files cannot be accessed without the link you received.
