Drop lets you quickly upload and share files

with your friends without registration!

The file size limit is 200 megabytes!

How it works

When you upload a document to Drop, you will receive a link that you can send to your friends and they will be able to download your document! But remember, the link will become invalid in 1 hour after upload so we can keep the service free and functional for everyone. No registration is required and you can upload documents that are not larger than 200 megabytes.

Upon uploading a document, the document will be scanned using the powerful VirusTotal engine, so you can be sure that an uploaded document does not contain malicious content.
